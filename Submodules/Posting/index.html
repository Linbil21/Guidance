ORIENTATION


Normally, orientation is held with all of the students together, however in this instance, transfer students and absentees can use online resources.

What for?

Student orientation is a vital introduction to academic life and their sense of belonging

Providing newcomers with essential information about campus resources, academic programs, and community involvement. It build a sense of belonging, acquaints students with policies, and helps them navigate their educational journey, ensuring a smooth transition into university life.

SCHOOL ORIENTATION

  • School orientations aren't usually scheduled; instead, you can sign up for them whenever it's almost time for the new semester. On the other hand, the timetables aren't set in stone, and they often only occur once or twice a year.

  • Student orientation is like a university roadmap. It shows newbies where everything is, introduces classes, and helps them fit into the college scene. It's a guide to kickstart their academic journey smoothly.

  • However, students can use the online resources from the previous orientation at no cost.

WORK ORIENTATION

  • You received an email with the work orientation schedule; it contains all the information you need to know about the position and what is expected of you, schedule and FAQs.

  • Work orientation is a job's welcome party. It introduces employes the culture, policies, and their roles. It's the starting point for a successful and harmonious work adventure.

  • The majority of the work that is advised is done inside the school, assisting with event planning and student administration. Those with position or who are familiar with the roles that their coworkers will play will do the orientation part.

Call Center

A call center is a centralized facility where a group of trained agents, often referred to as customer service representatives, handle incoming and outgoing telephone calls from customers. The primary purpose of a call center is to efficiently manage customer inquiries, provide support, address issues, and offer information or assistance related to products or services. Call centers may also use other communication channels, such as email, chat, or social media, to interact with customers based on the organization's communication strategy.

Work For The School

A school work-study program is an arrangement where students are employed by the school to perform various tasks, such as managing facilities and participating in activities, in exchange for financial compensation or scholarships. This initiative allows students to gain valuable work experience, contribute to the smooth functioning of the school, and earn financial support to fund their education. Read More

Work Online

An online academic support role involves working remotely with flexible hours, aiding professors and other school personnel to streamline their tasks through virtual assistance. This position focuses on leveraging technology to provide online support, making the workflow more efficient and contributing to the overall effectiveness of academic operations.
