ORIENTATION


Normally, orientation is held with all of the students together, however in this instance, transfer students and absentees can use online resources.

What for?

Student orientation is a vital introduction to academic life and their sense of belonging

Providing newcomers with essential information about campus resources, academic programs, and community involvement. It builds a sense of belonging, acquaints students with policies, and helps them navigate their educational journey.

Resources School Orientation Work Orientation Jobs

SCHOOL ORIENTATION

  • School orientations aren't usually scheduled; instead, you can sign up for them whenever it's almost time for the new semester.

  • Student orientation is like a university roadmap. It shows newbies where everything is, introduces classes, and helps them fit into the college scene.

  • However, students can use the online resources from the previous orientation at no cost.

WORK ORIENTATION

  • You received an email with the work orientation schedule; it contains all the information you need to know about the position and what is expected of you.

  • Work orientation is a job's welcome party. It introduces employees to the culture, policies, and their roles.

  • The majority of the work advised is done inside the school, assisting with event planning and student administration.
